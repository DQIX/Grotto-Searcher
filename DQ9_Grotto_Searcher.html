🔘 [QL] & [B9F]

  • [QL]Finds maps where 2-3 specified items can be looted on the same floor(B3F or B4F)immediately after performing a "Quickload"(Solo or Party).
  • [B9F]Finds maps where 2-3 S-Rank items can be looted on the B9F immediately after performing a "Quickload"(Solo or Party).
  • ⚠️ Note:Cannot be used to search for Trap Monsters(such as Cannibox),as defeating them refreshes the internal timer,ruining the map's farming viability.

🔘 [Combo] & [J-Fire]

  • [Combo]Find maps with following features:First,able to grab the main weapon via Quickload. Then,able to perform actions(open/close Battle Records,go upstairs/downstairs,etc.)to get the "3rd chest" item.
  • [J-Fire]Find maps for grabbing 2 Sainted Somas by the same method as above .

🔘 [3rd] & [S3]

  • Finds maps where two consecutive floors both have 3 chests. Grab the item from the first 3rd Chest,go downstairs,and immediately grab the next one.

💡 Glossary

Quickload(Party):With a full 4-member party,do a "Quick Save" at the target chest's floor. Reload the game andimmediately open the chest without doing anything else. Real time:7s(internal timer:2s).

Quickload(Solo):Same as above,but with only the Hero in the party. Real time:6s(internal timer:1s).

Millionaire:Maps containing multiple A/B rank chests that drop high-value weapons meant to be sold for gold.

3rd Chest:If a floor has3 chests,the 3rd chest has a unique RNG quirk:it will drop theexact same item for several consecutive seconds. This large time window guarantees a 100% success rate for obtaining the desired item!
